Peru

Peru is a South American country on the Pacific coast, defined by the Andes Mountains and the Amazon rainforest. It is famous for Machu Picchu and the legacy of the Inca civilization, as well as vibrant traditions that still thrive today. Peru is also known worldwide for its cuisine, especially dishes like ceviche and lomo saltado.
